We have long recognized that there are many jobs for DevOps positions and on the other hand many candidates without the knowledge required to be accepted. Therefore and accordingly, the Bakery wants to give head to the challenge

What's in the program:

  • The profession we chose to train - CI / CD
  • About 15 weekly sessions
  • Built-in mentoring
  • Knowledge sharing by industry and profession veterans
  • Practical workshops

The program is an initiative of good people in the middle of the road. Non-profit and small print. Everything on the table. If not understood from the previous sentence then the intent is that there is no commercial entity behind the initiative and it is at no cost to the participants. We want to share knowledge and help more quality people reach the professions of DevOps. And all this voluntarily.
