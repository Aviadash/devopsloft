We have long recognized that there are many vacant openings for DevOps positions and many willing talented candidates without the base knowledge required to be accepted. Therefore and accordingly, the Bakery coaching track wants to stand to the challenge!

What's in the program:

  • The profession we chose to train - CI/CD
  • About 15 weekly sessions
  • Built-in mentoring
  • Knowledge sharing by industry and profession veterans
  • Practical workshops

The program is an initiative of good people in the middle of the road. Non-profit and no small print. Everything is on the table. If not understood from the previous sentence, then the intent is that there is no commercial entity behind the initiative and it is at no cost to the participants. We want to share knowledge and help more quality people to reach the professions of DevOps. And all that is voluntarily.

Disclaimers:

    Do you think there are no small print? LoL!
  • It is not a course in the regular sense of the word
  • This is not a formal training
  • There is no certificate at the end
  • There is no guarantee that you will find a job in the DevOps field
  • There is no commitment to anything but good will !!!!
